About CPR Tech

Founded with a mission to simplify technology for businesses of all sizes, CPR Tech Support has grown into a trusted partner for companies seeking reliable IT solutions.

Our name reflects our commitment: just like CPR saves lives, we're here to revive and maintain the health of your technology infrastructure.

Devices: “Where It All Begins”

Every great system begins with a single connection — your laptop, your desktop, your printer.

These are the tools that bring your ideas to life and power your daily operations. When they slow down, so does your business. Reliable, well-maintained devices save time, reduce frustration, and keep your work moving forward — without costly downtime.

"We keep your devices healthy and your day running smoothly, from setup to support."

Networking: “The Pulse of Productivity”

Your network is the lifeblood of your business.

It connects your team, devices, and data — but when it’s unstable or poorly configured, everything grinds to a halt. A strong, well-managed network ensures your internet, printers, and file shares are always there when you need them. It’s the difference between constant disruptions and seamless collaboration.

"We design and maintain fast, reliable networks that keep your business connected — wired or wireless."

Servers & Directory Services: “The Brain of Your Business”

Behind the scenes, servers keep your world organised — managing files, users, logins, and updates.

They ensure the right people have the right access at the right time. If your business is growing, spreadsheets and shared folders can only go so far. Servers centralise your information and security, giving you control, consistency, and speed. It’s the foundation of a professional, scalable system that grows as you do. Whether it’s Active Directory, domain controllers, or deployment systems, we make sure it all just works.

"We build structured systems that make managing your business effortless."

Security & Firewalls: “Your Digital Immune System”

Every connection is a doorway — and not every visitor is welcome.

Cyber threats are no longer just for big companies; small businesses are often the easiest targets. Firewalls, antivirus, and layered security stop threats before they reach you, protecting your data, reputation, and customer trust. If your business relies on technology, security isn’t optional — it’s essential.

"We keep your systems secure so you can focus on what matters most: your work."

The Cloud & Internet: “The Lungs of Your System”

The cloud expands what’s possible — giving you access anywhere, anytime.

If you’re still tied to one computer or location, you’re limiting your flexibility. Cloud storage, backups, and collaboration tools keep your data safe, accessible, and always in sync. Whether you’re in the office or on the move, the cloud helps your business keep breathing — even when things go wrong.

"We connect you to cloud solutions that scale as you grow — safely and seamlessly."

The Whole System: “Breathe Life Into Your IT”

When everything works together — devices, networks, servers, security, cloud — your IT isn’t just functional, it’s alive.

Most tech issues aren’t random — they’re the result of one weak link in the chain. We strengthen every part of your system so it runs as one. When your technology works in harmony, your people can too.

"From single devices to complete infrastructures — we help you breathe life into your IT."
